Talent Book

Overview

The modeling industry is plagued by allegations of mistreatment of girls, lack of diversity, and sexual harassment, amongst many other issues. We created a mobile solution where models can search for castings/gigs/ that they can feel comfortable partaking in.

Role & Responsbilities + Team Size

My Responsibility on this project was to help gather user research and conduct one on one interviews. I also help sketch rapid prototypes and help develope the startups branding with the graphic design team.

User Research


Interviews

We gathered quantitative and qualitative data from multiple one-on-one interviews with models in the industry and mainly asked what their experiences have been and what can be improved. After the initial discussions, I did more user testing with each new version of the prototype and allowed the model to play around with the prototype developed in Figma.
