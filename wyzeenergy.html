Wyze Energy Drinks

Overview

Wyze Energy are making energy drinks that are healthy to consume. They achieve this by loading all of their cans with fresh fruits and vegetables.

Role & Responsbilities

My responsibility at Wyze Energy was to provide an updated and refreshing-looking website and develop their mobile application.

The Website

While developing the refreshed website for Wyze Energy, I worked closely with the design team to attach the new product images, nutrition labels, and critical marketing images to the website. I also helped update the search engine optimization to bring in new customers to the site. I also assisted in providing a packaging system to receive orders from customers accurately.

The App

The mobile app for Wyze Energy was quite an exciting project because we had to figure out to develop our in-house checkout system. I was in charge of developing the checkout system, which required tons of hands-on testing as we dealt with personal information such as credit cards and addresses. While we were conducting these tests, the most common error was that a credit card wasn’t going through the system, and our solution was to work with the IT department to allow the status code from the app to pass through.
